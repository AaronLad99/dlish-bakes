Can you recreate a cake from a photo?

We do not copy other bakers’ cakes. If you send an image for inspiration, we will design a cake in our own style that captures a similar look and feel.

Every cake is unique and finished with our own creative touch, so it will never be an exact replica.

Allergen Information

We work with common allergens in our kitchen, including milk, tree nuts, eggs, fish, soya and others.

Allergen-friendly cakes are prepared using separate mixing bowls, and all equipment and surfaces are thoroughly sanitised beforehand to minimise cross-contamination.

However, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment. Customers must inform us of any allergies when placing an order.

Do you accept last-minute orders?

We will always try to accommodate last-minute orders where availability allows.

A £10 last-minute fee applies.
