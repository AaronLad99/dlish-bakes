About me

Hi, I’m Alisha, a self-taught baker based in Birmingham.

I specialise in creating gluten-free, vegan, fondant cakes that are as delicious as they are visually striking. From custom themed celebration cakes to unique, one-of-a-kind designs, I love bringing creative ideas to life through baking. I’m always excited to try something new, whether that means experimenting with fresh flavours, innovative techniques, or bold themes, to ensure every cake is truly special and memorable.
